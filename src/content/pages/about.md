AI Sec Digest delivers a daily briefing on AI security news — breach disclosures, CVE coverage, jailbreak publications, regulatory actions, and tooling shifts, filtered for signal and sourced from primary documents.

## What we cover daily

- **Breach and incident disclosures** — AI/ML system incidents as they are reported, with primary-source links
- **CVE coverage** — vulnerabilities in ML frameworks and AI infrastructure, with severity context
- **Jailbreak and research releases** — new techniques disclosed by researchers and the AI safety community
- **Regulatory developments** — enforcement actions, guidance, and proposed legislation affecting AI
- **Tooling and research** — notable releases from the AI security ecosystem worth tracking

## Who reads this

Security practitioners, researchers, and executives who need to stay current on AI security without wading through vendor press releases and social media noise. Every item links to a primary source.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed or newsletter for the daily digest. Tips and story leads are welcome — contact the editorial desk with a primary-source link.
